Tajikistan is a landlocked country in Central Asia. About 93% of its territory is mountainous.
Tajik is the official language of Tajikistan, closely related to Persian (Farsi).
Tajikistan has a continental climate, with hot summers and very cold winters.
The economy of Tajikistan is highly dependent on agriculture, remittances, and aluminum production.
