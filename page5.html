Function 5: Dynamic Text Resizing

Tajikistan's history spans thousands of years, with the region playing a key role in ancient Persian empires, and later coming under the influence of Alexander the Great, Arab invaders, and the Mongols. In the 19th century, it became part of the Russian Empire, and after the Russian Revolution, Tajikistan was incorporated into the Soviet Union in 1929. Following the collapse of the Soviet Union, Tajikistan declared its independence in 1991. However, the country soon faced a devastating civil war from 1992 to 1997, which resulted in significant loss of life and economic hardship. The civil war left deep scars on the nation, but the subsequent peace agreement helped establish a fragile stability, allowing the country to slowly rebuild
