More About Tajikistan

Tajikistan, officially the Republic of Tajikistan, is a landlocked country in Central Asia. It is bordered by Afghanistan to the south, Uzbekistan to the west, Kyrgyzstan to the north, and China to the east. Its capital and largest city is Dushanbe.
